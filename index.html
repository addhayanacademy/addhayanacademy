ADDHYAN ACADEMY offers a plethora of programmes for all age groups . Our programmes establish strong foundations in maths , science , English and other subjects helping your child to feel confident enough to tackle challenging problems ..

Excellence is an attitude

Excellence is not a skill; it is an attitude is what we strongly believe in. Success is a journey and not a destination; we emulate this thought as we try to better ourselves with every possible opportunity to be of relevance and value for our students and their families. Students have to develop the habit of real-time learning and carry forward the knowledge to the broader spectrum to be successful in life and be truly global citizens.

CAREER COUNSELLING AND GUIDANCE:

At Addhyan Academy we provide complete career guidance to the students. We conduct stream selector test for grade 9 & 10 students. Psychometric test for career selection for grade 11 th & 12 th.

NSTSE, UCO & UIEO

ADDHYAN ACADEMY is also associated with UNIFIED COUNCIL HYDERABAD . We are authorised to conduct flagship competitions of Unified Council viz. NSTSE, UCO & UIEO. We also provide coaching for the same .

OLYMPIAD
